HONEST OVERVIEW

WHAT TO
EXPECT

We believe in transparency. Here's what online training does brilliantly — and where to be mindful.

✅
Unmatched Convenience
Train from your living room, a hotel room, or a park. No commute, no waiting for machines, no time wasted. Just you and your workout, on demand.
✅
Expert Access Worldwide
Access elite trainers in Tokyo, London, New York — all in one platform. Your local gym can't match the breadth of expertise available here.
✅
Go At Your Own Pace
Pause, rewind, and repeat without judgment. Our library is yours — whether you're a beginner or an advanced athlete, there's no pressure.
⚠️
Staying Motivated
Without a physical gym environment, motivation can dip. We combat this with live classes, streak tracking, community challenges, and accountability partners built into every plan.
⚠️
Limited Hands-On Correction
Online coaching can't physically adjust your form. We mitigate this with AI form analysis via your phone camera, detailed technique breakdowns, and optional video check-ins.
⚠️
Equipment Needs
Some classes need weights or resistance bands. We clearly label equipment requirements up front, and 30%+ of our library is fully bodyweight — no gear needed.
